If you are doing SEO and AEO research the old way, you need to give this a shot.

You take real questions and answer it with data instead of trying to buttonhole the existing structure into answering your questions.

✅Find content topics for a client by surfacing patterns across Reddit threads in their niche

✅Identify keywords that a competitor was losing rankings on that my client had a real shot at winning

✅Build a full internal linking structure by having it analyze how a site's content actually clusters

✅Create a PR outreach plan for a client who wanted brand visibility but had zero interest in chasing backlinks

None of these were use cases in the Ahrefs standard navigation; they were just questions I had.

If you haven't tried it, go do it now.
