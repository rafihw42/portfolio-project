## How Experts Were Selected

This list focuses on **practitioners**, not commentators. Each person here either:
- Publishes original research with real data and methodology,
- Operates an agency or product actively building AI content systems, or
- Has documented results (traffic growth, case studies, client outcomes) from AI content work.

Excluded: thought leaders who write about AI SEO but don't build or run the systems themselves; brand accounts (HubSpot, Semrush Blog) that produce content marketing, not practitioner insight.
